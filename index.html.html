💡 How to Keep Data Safe on Desktop

Your data saves automatically in the browser. To keep a permanent copy on your Desktop:

  1. Click Download Full Backup (.json) above
  2. Save the file to your Desktop or Documents folder
  3. Do this every week to keep data safe
  4. To restore: Click Restore from Backup and select your saved file
